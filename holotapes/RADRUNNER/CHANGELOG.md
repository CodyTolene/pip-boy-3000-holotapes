Changelog

All notable changes to RAD RUNNER are documented here.

[1.0.0] - 2026-06-22

Added


Initial release.
Endless three-lane wasteland runner with perspective road.
Jump, duck, shoot, and lane-dodge mechanics.
Obstacle set: radroach (jump), Atomic Cola sign (duck), ghoul (shoot),
wrecked car (dodge).
Distance-based scoring with a best-distance readout on game over.
Progressive difficulty: speed and spawn density increase over a run, with
full-width hazard walls at higher speeds.
Desert mesa skyline, generated fresh each launch.
Real elapsed-time game loop for consistent speed across frame rates.
Embedded sprite art (no external image files required).
